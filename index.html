Get started with AEVI AppFlow

Integrate your application with AppFlow by following one of our integration guides below. Make sure to also explore the rest of the documentation, javadocs and samples.

Flow initiation

Enables applications to query for AppFlow information and initiate flows for various functions such as sale, refund and tokenisation.

Value added applications

Provides the ability for applications (such as loyalty schemes) to read and augment data at different stages of the flow.

Payment applications

Allows payment applications to integrate with AppFlow and process payment related requests, using any payment method of their choice.

AppFlow compliance

Due to the flexible nature of AppFlow, it is important that your application deals with flows and data correctly. These pages assist in making sure your app is AppFlow compliant and play well with other applications.
